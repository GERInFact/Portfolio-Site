My journey of becoming a web developer

In 2019 I graduated at the SAE Institute Leipzig with a first class BSc.(Hons) in Games Programming, issued by the Middlesex University of London.

My commitment and my mentors great recommendation led me into a management role during my studies, which I had had to follow for 2 years.

October 2016 had been the year, I had the privilege of being appointed Director of Studies of Games Programming at the SAE Institute Leipzig. The second appointment was offered by the SAE Institute Hannover in 2017.

To keep up my momentum, I had ironed out a revised course program by January 2017. The amended course structures success drew the attention of international software companies of the likes of Crytek, Unity and Daedalic which rendered great opportunities for our students.

It was probably in one of those moments I sat down with Collin Bishop and Brian Dilg - some brilliant minds of Crytek - I've been encouraged to abandon my academic position to focus entirely on software engineering. Especially my profound knowledge of design principles like SOLID as well as clean code in general, led me into my present role as a software engineer at eder GmbH where I've taken part in designing and developing great full stack software solutions for Daimler, BMW, MAN and Mars.

Ever since I've realized the tremendous impact of scalable web solutions and their capability of minimizing OS restrictions as well as accessibility issues. This had to be the moment for my passions birth in web and cloud development and so I've continued my education with CareerFoundry to strengthen my skills in web development to proceed my career as freelancer, educator and prospective employee at your company.
