My journey of becoming a web developer

In 2019 I graduated at the SAE Institute Leipzig with a first class BSc.(Hons) in Games Programming, issued by the Middlesex University of London.

It is to bear in mind at this point of my story that I had the privilege of being appointed Director of Studies of the Games Programming course at the SAE Institute Leipzig in October 2016 followed accordingly of an additional appointment at the SAE Institute Hannover in 2017.

It was my commitment to the task at hand and my Mentors great recommendation that led me into a management role, which I had to follow for the next 2 years.

During this period I developed a revised course program, which drew the attention of international software companies of the likes of Crytek, Unity and Daedalic.

It was probably the moment I sat down with the brilliant minds of Collin Bishop and Brian Dilg, I was inspired to focus more and more on software engineering and especially on design principles as well as clean code that led me to my current software engineering role at eder GmbH where I've taken part in the development of great software solutions for Mars, BMW, Daimler and MAN.

Ever since I've realized the tremendous impact of web based software solutions, capable of minimizing OS restrictions and accessibility issues for clients that ignited my passion for web and cloud development.
